# Buildots (buildots)

Buildots is an AI-powered construction intelligence platform headquartered in Tel Aviv, Israel, with offices in London and the United States. The company combines 360-degree hardhat-mounted cameras with computer vision and machine learning models that automatically capture, locate, and classify on-site construction progress against the project BIM model and schedule, producing trade-level performance data, deviation and delay detection, and portfolio-wide analytics for general contractors, project teams, executives, and owners. Buildots reports that projects using the platform together with a performance-driven operating approach have reduced delays by up to 50%. The product family includes Buildots (the AI construction intelligence platform), Genda (workforce intelligence), and a fully managed Capture Services operation that handles routine site capture for customers. Verticals served include data centers, commercial, multifamily, healthcare, industrial, education, and airports. Notable customers shown on the website include Intel, Mortenson, JE Dunn, Turner, Wates, and STO. Buildots was founded in 2018 and is led by CEO and co-founder Roy Danon with co-founders Aviv Leibovici (CPO) and Yakir Sudry (CCO). Investors include Lightspeed Venture Partners, Future Energy Ventures, Viola Ventures, TLV Partners, OG Ventures, and Tencent. Buildots does not publish a public developer API, SDK, or developer portal; integrations with construction software (e.g., Procore, Autodesk Construction Cloud) are arranged directly through the company.

## Features

| Name | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 360-Degree Hardhat Capture | Site personnel wear a 360-degree camera mounted on a hardhat and walk the site on a routine cadence, automatically capturing imagery across every floor and area without disrupting their normal site walks. |
| AI Computer Vision Progress Tracking | Buildots' computer vision and ML models locate captured imagery against the floor plan, identify trade-level work in place, and compare it to the BIM model and project schedule to quantify progress automatically. |
| BIM and Schedule Integration | The platform ingests the project BIM model and construction schedule and uses them as the ground truth against which captured progress is measured, surfacing deviations and delays per trade and per area. |
| Trade-Level Performance Analytics | Buildots reports installed quantities and productivity per trade, enabling project teams and executives to track performance, manage subcontractors, and validate payment applications against actual work in place. |
| Delay and Risk Detection | AI models flag schedule slippage, sequencing issues, and high-risk areas early so project leaders can intervene before delays compound across downstream trades. |
| Deviation Detection | The system highlights installed work that deviates from the BIM model so quality, MEP coordination, and design-intent issues can be resolved while crews are still on site. |
| Portfolio-Wide Dashboards | Executives and operating-group leaders get cross-project dashboards that aggregate performance, risk, and progress across an entire portfolio, not just a single job. |
| Genda Workforce Intelligence | The Genda offering captures and analyzes workforce presence and productivity on site, providing labor analytics that complement the progress data from the core Buildots platform. |
| Capture Services | A fully managed service in which Buildots personnel perform the routine site captures on behalf of the customer, so general contractors get construction intelligence without having to staff the capture workflow themselves. |

## Use Cases

| Name | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Trade Management | Track subcontractor performance against the schedule per trade and per area, identify under-performing crews early, and run data-backed conversations with trade partners. |
| Delay Risk Mitigation | Detect schedule risk weeks in advance using AI-derived productivity and pace data, and trigger mitigation actions before downstream trades are impacted. |
| Deviation Detection | Compare installed work to the BIM model to surface MEP clashes, misplaced installations, and design-intent deviations while they are still inexpensive to fix. |

| Payment Applications | Validate subcontractor pay applications against measured installed quantities, reducing over-billing disputes and accelerating accurate payment cycles. |
